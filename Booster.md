Accelerates alien regeneration and provides them with
[poison](Poison "wikilink").

## Tips

- Touch a booster to temporarily make your attacks poisonous. Poison is
  great! Use it whenever you can, especially with a dretch.
- poison is also triggered when you fall on humans. With several aliens,
  simply jumping over a human's head will inoculate poison.
